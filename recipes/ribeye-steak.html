Ribeye Steak

Delicious Sloppy Steaks, doesn't get better than that.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Season steak
  3. Bake in oven for 10 minutes
  4. Remove from oven, sear each side on high for 1 minute
  5. Let rest 5 mins
  6. EAT
